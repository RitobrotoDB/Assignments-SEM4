Ordered List: Steps to Start a Garden

  1. Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost or fertilizer.
  2. Choose the plants you want to grow based on your climate and available space.
  3. Plan the layout of your garden, considering sunlight exposure and plant spacing.
  4. Purchase seeds or seedlings from a nursery or garden center.
  5. Plant the seeds or seedlings according to the instructions on the packaging.
